Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

konbu.tokyo Enterprise ("Developer") provides the "Sudoku Cookie" mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect the following information:

1.1 Account Information (When Using Google Sign-In)

  • Google account email address
  • Google account display name
  • Google account profile picture URL
  • Google authentication user ID

1.2 Game Data

  • Game scores (points, clear time)
  • Difficulty settings
  • Play history
  • Ranking data
  • Run-away count (number of times you quit a game)

1.3 Device Information

  • Game settings stored in local storage
  • Anonymous local user ID (when not using Google Sign-In)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing game features (score saving, ranking display)
  • User authentication and account management
  • Displaying global rankings
  • Saving and restoring game settings
  • Improving the App and fixing bugs

3. Where We Store Your Information

The collected information is stored in the following locations:

  • Device Local Storage: Game settings, local score history
  • Firebase Cloud Firestore: Global ranking data, user authentication information

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

4.1 Google Sign-In

  • Purpose: User authentication
  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Firebase (Google Cloud)

  • Purpose: Database, authentication services
  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • When you have given your consent
  • When required to comply with legal obligations
  • With third-party services necessary to provide App features (see Section 4)

6. Data Deletion

You can delete the following data from the App's settings screen:

  • Local score history
  • Remote data (scores and run-away history stored in Firestore)

If you wish to disconnect your Google account, you can revoke the App's access permissions from your Google Account settings.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, we cannot guarantee that data transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

8. Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will promptly delete it.

9. Advertising

Currently, the App does not display advertisements. If we introduce advertising in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify users.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will post the new Privacy Policy on this page and update the "Last Updated" date. We recommend checking this page periodically.
